How it works

Up and running in 2 minutes

1

Download and connect Apple Health

VitalDash reads your existing sleep, HRV and heart rate data. No wearable required — though Apple Watch gives you the richest data.

2

See your Burnout Index instantly

The algorithm calculates your score from 5 weighted metrics. No setup. No questionnaires. Just open the app and see your number.

3

Get your AI recovery insight

Every day you get a personalised 2-3 sentence recommendation based on your actual health data — not a generic tip.

4

Track your trends over time

30 days of history stored locally on your iPhone. See your burnout patterns and watch your recovery improve week over week.
