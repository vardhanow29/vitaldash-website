How it works

Up and running
in two minutes

01
Apple Health

Connect once, run forever

VitalDash reads your existing sleep, HRV and heart rate from Apple Health. No wearable required — though Apple Watch gives the richest data.

02
Algorithm

See your Burnout Index instantly

Five weighted metrics combine into one honest score every morning — sleep deficit, HRV trend, resting HR, activity load and sleep consistency.

03
AI

Get your personalised insight

Every day a 2–3 sentence recovery recommendation based on your real numbers — not a generic tip copied from a wellness blog.

04
History

Watch yourself recover over time

30 days of history stored locally on your iPhone. See burnout patterns, track your recovery arc and build lasting resilience.
