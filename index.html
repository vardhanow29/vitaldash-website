How it works

Up and running
in two minutes

01
Apple Health

Connect once, run forever

VitalDash reads your existing sleep, HRV and heart rate data from Apple Health. No wearable required — though Apple Watch unlocks the richest insights.

02
Algorithm

See your Burnout Index instantly

Five weighted metrics — sleep deficit, HRV trend, resting heart rate, activity load and sleep consistency — combine into one honest score every morning.

03
AI

Get your personalised insight

Every day you receive a 2–3 sentence recovery recommendation based on your real numbers — not a generic tip copied from a wellness blog.

04
History

Watch yourself recover over time

30 days of trend history stored locally on your iPhone. See burnout patterns, track your recovery arc, and build lasting resilience week over week.
